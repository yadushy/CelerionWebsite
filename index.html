Our mission

Every day, healthcare teams across India make critical decisions under pressure — with limited time, high workloads and incomplete information.
We build AI that makes those decisions faster, safer and better.

Celerion is an AI health company building a suite of clinical decision support tools for doctors, nurses and all emergency healthcare staff across India. Our tools are designed to work alongside people — not replace them.
