About us

Building AI for the people who keep people alive

Celerion Health is an AI company on a mission to improve clinical decision making across India.

Who we are

Celerion Health is an AI health company building clinical decision support tools for doctors, nurses and emergency healthcare teams across India.

We believe that better information leads to better decisions — and better decisions save lives. Our tools are designed to work alongside clinicians, providing structured, evidence-based support at the point of care.

We are at the early stage of building a broader platform of AI tools, each addressing a specific clinical workflow. Our first product focuses on emergency triage support, with more tools in development.

Why India

India's healthcare system faces unique challenges — high patient volumes, diverse disease patterns including tropical and endemic conditions, and varying levels of resource availability across regions.

Generic AI tools built for Western healthcare systems don't account for India's disease landscape. We build tools that understand the local context — from dengue and malaria to the high prevalence of diabetes and hypertension.

Our tools are built to work in real Indian emergency departments — fast, offline-resilient and designed for the actual pressures of the job.

What we believe

Our principles

Four principles guide every product decision we make.

01
AI assists, clinicians decide

Our tools reduce cognitive load and support better judgement. The final decision always belongs to the care team. We will never position our tools as a replacement for clinical expertise.

02
India-first

Built for the diseases, conditions and healthcare contexts that matter most in India. Not adapted from somewhere else — designed from the ground up for Indian healthcare.

03
Speed without compromise

Clinical AI is only useful if it keeps up with clinical reality. Our tools are fast by design — minimal inputs, instant structured outputs, built for the floor not the office.

04
Privacy as standard

Patient data is never an afterthought. All data stays on device. No external servers, no third-party sharing. Security and privacy are built into every product from day one.
